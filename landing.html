About Us

At TechAfrica Connect, our project was inspired by the desire to bridge the digital divide in Africa by providing innovative solutions to connect people, communities, and businesses. Our journey began with a shared vision of leveraging technology to empower individuals and organizations across the continent.

As a Portfolio Project for Holberton School, we embarked on this journey with passion and determination to create a platform that fosters collaboration, facilitates knowledge sharing, and drives socio-economic development in Africa.
